About CGEC Club
A student-run engineering and cybersecurity community — founded 2022

CGEC Club is a dynamic, purpose-driven community where technology, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence come together to foster learning, collaboration, and hands-on practice.


We empower members to develop real-world IT skills while driving innovation, strengthening digital security, and using technology to create meaningful impact in their community. Anyone enrolled at the institution can join — first-years to final-year.
